Turner Residence

Project Principals

Architecture: Nievera Williams Design, Inc.

The formal indoor pool at the Turner Residence recalls the grandeur of the turn of the last century when guests would gather in their swimming costumes for mid-day cocktails and an exchange of witticisms.

The New Abbey Limestone carries this elegance with aplomb; its subtle variation creating intra-stone movement that supports the scene without ever upstaging the hostess. Walking surfaces and pool copings received a light sandblast finish to keep the gathering safe and injury free while walls, columns and moldings were hand-dressed to a honed but non-reflective surface. This allows the refracted light to warm, rather than bounce from the stone to those who enjoy the splendor of the pool and environs.
