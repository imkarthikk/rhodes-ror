Madison Lake Front

Project Principals

Architect: Olson, Sundberg, Kundig & Allen Architects

Interior Design & Architecture: Saladino Group

Landscape Architect: Deborah Nevins Associates

Situated on the shores of Lake Washington this recently completed home is in keeping with the traditional 19th century look of many East Coast estates.

Silk Road Sandstone surrounds the grand entry, which leads to the Antique Granite staircase inside.

Antique Salmon Granite pavements have been gauged for an innovative wall treatment in the kitchen.

In the garden Antique Pearl Limestone harmonizes with Antique Granite stair treads to create a tranquil atmosphere.
