Why Rhodes

Our customers are architects, designers, and individuals who seek to translate their artistic vision into the medium of stone. Our customers understand from the outset that RAS is a company run by and for creative professionals.

RAS Advantages:

  • – 20 years of experience in quarrying, stone design,
    fabrication, and installation
  • – Technical stone expertise and best practices recommendations
  • – Industry leading stone expertise
  • – finest new and antique materials
  • – Hand Crafted Artisan Finishes
  • – Comprehensive shop drawings
  • – Experienced in-country teams
  • – Rapid mockup capability
  • – Lowest installed cost
